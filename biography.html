Biography

      Hi again, my name is Yağmur Buse. I am 21 years old. I was born in Siirt. I am from Malatya. Now, me and my family live in Elazığ. A few months later we will move to another city, Amasya. Because of my father's job we must moving city to city. My father is a soldier. My mother is housewife. I have two brother. I am the biggest child in our family.

     I went to primary school in Diyarbakır. Then, we moved to another city and I went to middle school in Tokat. My first and second high school years were spent in Amasya. Five years later we moved to Elazığ and finally I graduated in Elazığ Ahmet Yesevi High School and finished high school. Now, I am studying at Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University and my department is Management Information Systems.
